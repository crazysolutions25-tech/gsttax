Rules & Regulations

Consolidated reference for GST, Income Tax and TDS rules, due dates, penalties and compliance requirements.

1. GST Rules & Compliance

GST Registration Rules

  • Mandatory registration if turnover exceeds prescribed threshold
  • Compulsory registration for inter-state suppliers
  • E-commerce operators require mandatory GST registration
  • Registration to be obtained within 30 days of becoming liable

GST Return Filing Rules

  • Returns must be filed even if there are no transactions (NIL return)
  • Input tax credit can be claimed only on valid invoices
  • Late filing attracts late fees and interest

GST Payment Rules

  • GST liability must be paid before filing GSTR-3B
  • Interest at 18% per annum applies on delayed payment

2. GST Due Dates

Return Due Date
GSTR-1 (Monthly) 11th of next month
GSTR-3B 20th of next month
GSTR-4 (Composition) 30th April
GSTR-9 (Annual) 31st December

3. GST Penalties & Late Fees

  • ₹50 per day late fee for delayed returns
  • ₹20 per day for NIL returns
  • Penalty for non-registration may apply
  • Blocking of e-way bill generation for non-filers

4. Income Tax Rules

Income Tax Return Filing Rules

  • ITR filing mandatory if income exceeds exemption limit
  • Companies and firms must file returns irrespective of income
  • ITR must be verified within prescribed time

Income Tax Due Dates

Category Due Date
Individuals (Non-Audit) 31st July
Audit Cases 31st October
Transfer Pricing 30th November

Income Tax Penalties

  • Late filing fee under section 234F
  • Interest under sections 234A, 234B and 234C
  • Loss of carry forward of losses

5. TDS Rules & Compliance

TDS Deduction Rules

  • TDS must be deducted at applicable rates
  • TDS deducted must be deposited within due dates
  • TDS certificates must be issued to deductees

TDS Return Due Dates

Quarter Due Date
Q1 31st July
Q2 31st October
Q3 31st January
Q4 31st May

TDS Penalties

  • Late fee of ₹200 per day under section 234E
  • Penalty from ₹10,000 to ₹1,00,000 under section 271H
  • Interest on delayed TDS payment

6. Importance of Compliance

Compliance with GST, Income Tax and TDS rules is essential to avoid penalties, interest and legal complications. Regular filing, timely payments and accurate record maintenance help ensure smooth business operations and financial stability.
