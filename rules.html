Late Fees & Penalties

  • GST Late Fee: ₹50 per day (₹20 for NIL return)
  • Interest on GST Delay: 18% per annum
  • Income Tax Late Filing Penalty: Up to ₹5,000
  • TDS Late Fee: ₹200 per day
