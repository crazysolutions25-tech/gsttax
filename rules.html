Rules & Regulations – Complete Guide

A comprehensive guide to GST, Income Tax and TDS rules, due dates, penalties and compliance requirements in India.

1. GST Rules & Regulations

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is governed by the CGST Act, SGST Act and IGST Act. Every registered taxpayer must comply with registration, return filing, payment and record-keeping rules.

GST Registration Rules

  • Mandatory registration if turnover exceeds threshold limits
  • Compulsory registration for inter-state suppliers
  • Voluntary registration allowed below threshold

GST Return Filing Rules

  • Returns must be filed even if there is no business (NIL return)
  • Late filing attracts late fees and interest
  • Continuous non-filing can lead to cancellation of GST registration

2. GST Return Due Dates

Return Due Date
GSTR-1 (Monthly) 11th of next month
GSTR-3B 20th of next month
GSTR-4 (Composition) 30th April
GSTR-9 (Annual) 31st December

3. GST Penalties & Interest

  • Late fee: ₹50 per day (₹20 per day for NIL returns)
  • Interest: 18% per annum on tax due
  • Penalty for non-registration: minimum ₹10,000 or 10% of tax due

4. Income Tax Rules

Income Tax in India is governed by the Income Tax Act, 1961. Individuals, firms and companies must file income tax returns if income exceeds the basic exemption limit.

Income Tax Return Due Dates

Category Due Date
Individuals (Non-Audit) 31st July
Businesses (Audit cases) 31st October

Income Tax Penalties

  • Late filing fee up to ₹5,000 under section 234F
  • Interest under sections 234A, 234B, 234C

5. TDS Rules

Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is applicable to specified payments such as salary, rent, professional fees, and contractor payments.

TDS Return Due Dates

Quarter Due Date
Q1 (Apr–Jun) 31st July
Q2 (Jul–Sep) 31st October
Q3 (Oct–Dec) 31st January
Q4 (Jan–Mar) 31st May

TDS Penalties

  • Late fee: ₹200 per day (maximum equal to TDS amount)
  • Penalty for non-filing: ₹10,000 to ₹1,00,000
