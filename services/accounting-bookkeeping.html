Accounting & Bookkeeping – Complete Guide

Detailed explanation of accounting and bookkeeping services, statutory requirements, records maintenance and compliance support.

1. What is Accounting?

Accounting is the systematic process of recording, classifying, summarizing and interpreting financial transactions of a business. It provides a clear picture of the financial position and performance of an entity and helps in decision-making.

2. What is Bookkeeping?

Bookkeeping is the process of recording day-to-day financial transactions such as sales, purchases, receipts and payments. Proper bookkeeping ensures that financial records are accurate and up to date.

3. Importance of Accounting & Bookkeeping

  • Accurate financial records
  • Compliance with statutory requirements
  • Preparation of financial statements
  • Better financial planning and control
  • Support during audits and assessments

4. Accounting & Bookkeeping Services Covered

  • Recording of daily transactions
  • Ledger maintenance
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Preparation of trial balance
  • Preparation of profit & loss account
  • Balance sheet preparation
  • GST accounting and reconciliation
  • TDS accounting and compliance

5. Who Needs Accounting & Bookkeeping Services?

  • Small and medium businesses
  • Startups and entrepreneurs
  • Professionals such as doctors, consultants and freelancers
  • Partnership firms, LLPs and companies

6. Accounting Records to be Maintained

  • Sales and purchase registers
  • Cash book and bank book
  • Expense records
  • Inventory records (where applicable)
  • GST and TDS records

7. Statutory Compliance Linked with Accounting

  • GST return filing
  • Income tax return filing
  • TDS return filing
  • Audit compliance

8. Consequences of Improper Accounting

  • Incorrect financial statements
  • Tax demand notices
  • Penalties and interest
  • Difficulty during audits

9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is bookkeeping mandatory for all businesses?
Yes, maintaining books of accounts is mandatory under various laws.

Q: Can accounting be outsourced?
Yes, businesses often outsource accounting for accuracy and cost efficiency.

Q: How often should books be updated?
Books should be updated regularly, preferably on a monthly basis.
